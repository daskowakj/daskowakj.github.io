Secondary

The primary function of the urinary bladder is to store and periodically eliminate urine. During clinical and whole animal assessments of urinary bladder function, it is difficult to predict when the bladder will switch between storage and elimination modes. Defining the behavior of variables collected during common urodynamics procedures to the time point where the bladder switches between modes will help increase the predictive validity of future models of the bladder switching mechanism.
